About Daksh Hande

Daksh Hande is a trailblazing professional revolutionizing the aviation industry with his innovative approach and leadership. His passion for excellence drives him to set new standards in aviation management and customer experience.

As a serial entrepreneur, Daksh founded RetroWeb.space, a news blog that achieved over 300 visitors shortly after its launch in 2025. Though the venture faced revenue challenges, it honed his skills in digital marketing, content creation, and business strategy.

With a vision for a better future, Daksh is laying the groundwork for the Daksh Foundation, set to launch in 2027. The foundation will focus on education and environmental sustainability, creating lasting impact in Western countries.

In his personal life, Daksh is an avid traveler, aviation enthusiast, and advocate for lifelong learning, constantly seeking new ways to grow and inspire others.

Philanthropic Vision

Daksh Foundation (Launching 2027)

The Daksh Foundation, launching in 2027, aims to transform lives through targeted initiatives in:

  • Education: Providing scholarships, learning resources, and mentorship programs for underprivileged youth in Western countries.
  • Environment: Supporting reforestation, renewable energy projects, and climate education to promote sustainability.
  • Community Empowerment: Fostering local leadership and economic opportunities through skill-building workshops.

Operating exclusively in Western countries, the foundation will leverage strategic partnerships to maximize impact. Join us in building a brighter, greener future.
