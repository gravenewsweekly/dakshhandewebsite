Meet Daksh Hande

“Innovation and impact are the cornerstones of progress. I’m here to build a better future.”

— Daksh Hande

Daksh Hande is a distinguished aviation professional renowned for his expertise in operational excellence, safety protocols, and customer-focused solutions. With a career spanning high-stakes aviation management, Daksh has earned accolades for driving efficiency and innovation.

As an entrepreneur, he launched RetroWeb.space, a digital news platform that achieved over 300 unique visitors within its first month in 2025. Though discontinued due to funding constraints, it showcased his prowess in digital strategy, SEO, and audience engagement.

Looking ahead, Daksh is preparing to launch the Daksh Foundation in 2027, a nonprofit dedicated to advancing education and environmental sustainability in Western countries. His vision is to empower communities through scholarships, renewable energy initiatives, and climate education.
