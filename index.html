About Daksh

"Innovation and impact are my driving forces. I aim to leave a legacy through aviation, entrepreneurship, and philanthropy."

— Daksh Hande

Daksh Hande is a dynamic aviation professional with a passion for leadership and innovation. Currently excelling in the aviation industry, he specializes in operational efficiency, safety standards, and customer service excellence.

As an entrepreneur, Daksh founded RetroWeb.space, a news blog that attracted over 300 visitors in its first month. Though the venture was discontinued due to financial challenges, it honed his skills in digital marketing, content creation, and business strategy.

Looking ahead, Daksh is preparing to launch the Daksh Foundation in 2027, which will focus on education and environmental initiatives in Western countries. His vision is to create lasting change through sustainable and impactful programs.

  • Got into aviation industry for excellence in aviation management (2025).
  • Founded RetroWeb.space, achieving rapid audience growth.
  • Committed to launching a philanthropy foundation by 2027.

Future Philanthropy

Daksh Foundation (Launching 2027)

The Daksh Foundation will launch in 2027 with a mission to drive impactful change in two core areas:

  • Education: Providing scholarships, learning resources, and mentorship programs for underprivileged students in Western countries.
  • Environment: Supporting conservation projects, renewable energy initiatives, and climate education to promote sustainability.

Focused exclusively on Western countries, the foundation aims to maximize its impact through targeted programs and strategic partnerships. Stay tuned for updates as we approach the launch!
