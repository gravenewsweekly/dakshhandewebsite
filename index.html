About Daksh

Daksh Hande is a dynamic professional currently making significant contributions in the aviation industry. With a passion for innovation and leadership, Daksh has demonstrated exceptional skills in his field.

Born with an entrepreneurial spirit, Daksh previously founded and served as CEO of RetroWeb.space, a news blog website that quickly gained traction with over 300 visitors by March of its launch year. Despite its promising start, the venture had to be discontinued due to revenue challenges, though it remains a valuable learning experience in Daksh's professional journey.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Daksh is preparing to launch the Daksh Foundation in 2027, focusing on education and environmental causes in Western countries.

Future Philanthropy

Daksh Foundation (Launching 2027)

The Daksh Foundation will be established in 2027 with a mission to create meaningful impact in two key areas:

  • Education: Focused on providing access to quality education and learning resources for underprivileged communities in Western countries.
  • Environment: Committed to environmental conservation projects, sustainable development initiatives, and climate change awareness programs.

The foundation will operate exclusively in Western countries, with no current plans for operations in India. This strategic focus allows for concentrated impact in regions where the foundation can maximize its effectiveness.
