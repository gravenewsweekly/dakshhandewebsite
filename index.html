About Daksh Hande

“My mission is to inspire change through innovation, leadership, and social impact.”

— Daksh Hande

Daksh Hande is a trailblazing aviation professional with a track record in operational ground staff, safety, and customer-centric solutions. His passion for innovation drives his work in the aviation industry, where he consistently delivers results.

As an entrepreneur, Daksh founded RetroWeb.space, a digital news platform that gained over 300 unique visitors in its first month. Though discontinued due to funding challenges, it sharpened his expertise in digital strategy, SEO, and business development.

With a vision for global impact, Daksh is set to launch the Daksh Foundation in 2027, focusing on education and environmental sustainability in Western countries. His commitment to creating meaningful change defines his legacy.

  • Recognized for excellence in aviation management (2025).
  • Founded RetroWeb.space, achieving rapid audience growth.
  • Preparing to launch the Daksh Foundation by 2027.

Philanthropic Vision

Daksh Foundation (Est. 2027)

The Daksh Foundation, launching in 2027, will empower communities through:

  • Education: Offering scholarships, resources, and mentorship to underprivileged students in Western countries.
  • Environment: Funding conservation, renewable energy, and climate education for a sustainable future.

With a focus on Western countries, the foundation will partner with global organizations to maximize impact. Join us in building a brighter future.
