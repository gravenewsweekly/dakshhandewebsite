A Vision for Global Impact

Transforming lives through education and environmental sustainability starting in 2027.

Our Philanthropic Mission

Daksh Hande is committed to creating a meaningful impact through strategic philanthropy. Starting in 2027, his initiatives will focus on two key areas to drive systemic change.

Empowering Education

Supporting innovative programs in Western countries to provide equitable access to quality education, resources, and scholarships.

Preserving Our Planet

Funding conservation efforts, renewable energy projects, and climate change solutions for a sustainable future.

Roadmap to 2027 and Beyond

Daksh’s philanthropy is strategically planned to ensure maximum impact. Here’s the timeline for launching his initiatives.

2025–2026

Research & Partnerships

Identifying high-impact organizations and programs in education and environmental sectors for collaboration.

2027

Launch Initiatives

Begin funding selected programs, with a focus on measurable outcomes in Western countries.

2028+

Foundation Creation

Establish a formal foundation to scale efforts and sustain long-term impact globally.

Inspired by Global Leaders

Daksh draws inspiration from organizations like the Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation, known for its strategic approach to environmental advocacy.

His vision is to blend innovative philanthropy with actionable outcomes, creating a legacy of positive change.
