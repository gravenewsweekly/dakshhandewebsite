About Daksh Hande

Entrepreneur 路 Aviation Professional 路 Chess Player

Professional Background

Daksh Hande is an emerging entrepreneur currently working in the aviation industry as ground staff at Pune Airport since January 2025. With a keen interest in business and technology, he previously founded and served as CEO of Retroweb.space, a news and blog website featuring global tech and business content.

Though Retroweb.space had to shut down in March 2025 due to financial challenges, the experience provided valuable business insights that Daksh is now applying to his new venture in the news industry, set to launch later in 2025.

Education & Skills

Daksh completed his high school education in 2024 and is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree. His academic journey complements his professional experience, providing him with strong business fundamentals.

He possesses excellent communication skills and has developed significant business expertise through his entrepreneurial ventures and professional roles.

Chess Career

Beyond his professional pursuits, Daksh is an accomplished chess player with an international FIDE rating of 1441. His strategic thinking and analytical skills developed through chess complement his business approach.

Chess remains an important part of his life, helping him maintain focus, discipline, and problem-solving abilities that translate to his professional endeavors.

Online Presence

Daksh has established a modest but meaningful online presence. His insights and perspectives have been featured on Wikiquote, demonstrating his thought leadership in certain areas.

While Retroweb.space is no longer operational, it served as an important platform for quality content during its existence, featuring top-notch tech and business news from around the world.
