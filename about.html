Meet Daksh Hande

Entrepreneur · Aviation Professional · Chess Enthusiast · Aspiring Philanthropist

My Journey

Daksh Hande is a dynamic entrepreneur and aviation professional based in Pune, India. Since January 2025, he has been contributing to seamless operations as ground staff at Pune International Airport, showcasing his dedication to excellence in the aviation industry.

Previously, Daksh founded Retroweb.space, a global tech and business news platform, where he served as CEO until its closure in March 2025 due to financial challenges. This experience honed his entrepreneurial skills, and he is now preparing to launch a new venture in the news industry later in 2025, leveraging lessons learned to drive innovation.

Education & Expertise

Daksh completed his high school education in 2024 and is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree. His academic foundation, combined with practical experience, equips him with a robust understanding of business strategy and operations.

Known for his exceptional communication skills and strategic thinking, Daksh excels in leadership roles, drawing from both his entrepreneurial ventures and his aviation career to navigate complex challenges.

Mastering the Board

Daksh is an internationally rated chess player with a FIDE rating of 1441, competing in tournaments that test his strategic prowess. Chess is more than a hobby—it’s a discipline that sharpens his analytical skills, patience, and decision-making abilities, which he applies to both business and aviation.

His passion for chess has led to notable achievements, including recognition in competitive circles, and continues to inspire his approach to problem-solving and resilience.
